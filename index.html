NOTICE The Spokane Islamic Center requires the following if hosting during Ramadan:
  1. You must bring food for breaking fast in addition to dinner (beverages, dates, and water).
  2. You must bring plates, cups, and utensils.
  3. The kitchen may only be used for heating food, not for cooking.
  4. You must clean the kitchen and the masjid after hosting.
  5. You must provide food and beverages for at least 70 people.
  6. Seven days before your scheduled hosting date, you will receive a text message for confirmation. If you do not confirm, your date may be given to someone else.
  7. The masjid will be open starting from Asr. Please bring the food at least 45 minutes before Maghrib.
  8. No stored food or leftover food may be left behind.
  9. Please bring your own serving spoons and make sure not to leave any dishes behind. The masjid will not be responsible for lost items.
